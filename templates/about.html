03 : About me

My Bio

I am a Tech enthusiast. I love programming because of its wide range of use in automation. I started my career very weakly in 2018 where I enrolled with a web developmwnt tutor for a period of 5 days. There, I learnt the basics of HTML and CSS before I left. After then, I had no chance to continue what I had passion for. This makes me think deep everyday. Fortunately, I got admission into a renown Univesity in Nigeria, Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife into department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering in the same 2018. Though it is not Computer Science but we do a lot of programming in our department. We resumed in the mid 2019 and from then I have been trying to walk along the path of my aim, which is to become a developer...

Education History

I attended Al-Kitab Model School, Kisi during my first five years in life. Then I moved on to Ibadan to study in Islamic Academy, Oja'ba. I left there when I got to Primary 3. Then I started to attend Muslim Association of Nigeria, MAN, Nursery and Primary school, Oke-Ado, where I sat for my common entrance examination into Secondary Schools in Oyo State in 2012. Then I got admission into Muslim Model College Ibadan, MMCI, in 2012. Afterall, it is in the same compound with my last primary school I attended. I used a period of 6 years in the school and I graduated as the Senior Prefect of 2017/2018 session in 2018. Fortunately later in the year 2018, I got admission into Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife into the department of Electrical Electronics Engineering and we resumed session in April 2019.
